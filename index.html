About

Elizabeth Efeelobari Letam is an automation specialist and technical writer who builds intelligent systems on the Google Workspace ecosystem and translates their complexity into language anyone can understand.

With a foundation in Medical Laboratory Science and published journalism spanning technology, health, law, and business, Elizabeth occupies a rare position: she builds the tools and writes the words that make them matter.

Her practice is defined by one principle: information should not just function, it should be understood. Every system she ships comes with four outputs, the code, a reference document, a technical article, and a beginner annotation book.

Her systems serve multi-location boutiques, logistics companies, property managers, and food businesses. Her journalism has reached hundreds of thousands of readers across Africa and beyond. Her peer-reviewed research has added original evidence to the global Mpox vaccine literature.
